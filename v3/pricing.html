// METHODOLOGY

Filament weight — estimated print weight (in grams) based on typical 3D-printed fidget designs at 15-20% infill. Will vary ±20% depending on size variant chosen.

Filament cost — calculated at AUD $0.030/g, the going rate for premium PLA+ at Australian retailers (~$30/kg). Excludes electricity (~$0.50/h × print hours), printer wear, packaging, time.

Competitor pricing — researched from Etsy AU, Fun Fidgets, Kaiko Fidgets, Sensory Tools Australia, The Sensory Store, Sensory Assist and Amazon AU on 2026-04-26. Low = entry-level seller, Avg = median list price across 5+ similar products, High = premium variant (large size / metal / glow / collector tier).

Our price — set at competitor average per your directive, rounded to the nearest dollar. This is mid-market; Tom can shift up if word-of-mouth supports premium positioning, or down to undercut.

Sourced item — the Sensory Fidget Blanket is sewn fabric, not 3D-printed. It's listed for completeness but the production economics are different — Tom would either buy wholesale and resell, or source handmade.
