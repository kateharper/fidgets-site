From CAD
to your pocket.

Every fidget begins as a parametric model in Fusion 360 and ends as something small enough to lose in a pencil case. In between sits a five-step process: design, slice, print, test, ship. Nothing leaves the studio that hasn't been spun, clicked or twisted by hand.

№ 05 — QUESTIONS

Frequently asked.

Are these allowed at school? +

Most are. Stick to the quiet ones (sliders, cubes, articulated creatures). Spinners get noisy if you go full power, so use indoor voice.

How do I pay? +

Online: Apple Pay, Google Pay or card via Stripe. In person at school: tap on the Square Terminal.

Can I pick a colour? +

Yes — every fidget has 4–6 colour options. Glow-in-the-dark is +$2.

What if it breaks? +

Bring it back — print failures are replaced free. Scooter accidents are on you.

Custom orders? +

Email me. If I can model it, I can print it. Bulk orders get a discount.
