HOW IT'S MADE

From CAD to your pocket.

Every fidget is designed, printed, and quality-checked in Sydney. We use premium PLA+ and PETG filament for parts that snap, click and spin like they should.

  • 01 Modelled in Fusion 360 with print-in-place hinges and joints.
  • 02 Printed at 0.16 mm layer height for buttery smooth surfaces.
  • 03 Hand-tested before it ships. If it doesn't spin right, it doesn't go out.
