// PROCESS

From CAD to your pocket.

Every fidget cycles through a five-step pipeline before it ships.

  1. 01 / DESIGN
    Modelled in Fusion 360 with print-in-place hinges & joints.
  2. 02 / SLICE
    Tuned slicing profiles per material — PLA+ for impact, PETG for flex.
  3. 03 / PRINT
    0.16 mm layers on a calibrated Bambu X1C. Smooth surfaces, tight tolerances.
  4. 04 / TEST
    Spin, click, twist, drop. If it doesn't pass, it gets reprinted.
  5. 05 / SHIP
    Hand-packed and posted Sydney-wide. Schoolyard pickup at recess.
