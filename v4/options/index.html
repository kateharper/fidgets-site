// HEY SIMON

Three takes on the premium tier.

All three options reuse the v4 INCYT design and the full 30-product regular catalogue (3 loss-leaders, 22 regular, 5 premium). The only thing that varies is what's in the premium tier and how you check out.

Confirmed legal facts (NSW Weapons Prohibition Act 1998 Schedule 1): butterfly knife / balisong = a "blade or spike between 2 handles" — prohibited weapon, up to 14 years max penalty. The form factor is what's banned, not the edge. So actual metal butterflies (sharp, blunt, or trainer) are off the table in all three options. Butterfly combs (Simon's idea — comb teeth instead of blade) are NOT covered by Schedule 1 and are included in all three options.
