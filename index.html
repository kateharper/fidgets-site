How it's made

From CAD to your pocket.

Every fidget is designed, printed, and quality-checked in Sydney. We use premium PLA+ and PETG filament for parts that snap, click and spin like they should — and won't shatter when they hit the school floor.

  • 01 Modelled in Fusion 360 with print-in-place hinges and joints.
  • 02 Printed at 0.16mm layer height for buttery smooth surfaces.
  • 03 Hand-tested before it ships. If it doesn't spin right, it doesn't go out.

Quick answers

FAQ.

Are these allowed at school? +

Most are. Stick to the quiet ones (sliders, cubes, articulated creatures) and you're sweet. Spinners get noisy if you go full power, so use indoor voice.

How do I pay? +

Online: Apple Pay, Google Pay or card via Stripe. In person at school: tap your card or phone on the Square Terminal. Done in seconds.

Can I pick a colour? +

Yes — every fidget has 4-6 colour options. Glow-in-the-dark is +$2 and worth it.

What if it breaks? +

Bring it back — if it's a print failure I'll replace it free. If you ran it over with a scooter, that's on you.

Custom orders? +

Email me. If I can model it, I can print it. Bulk orders for class gifts or birthday parties get a discount.
